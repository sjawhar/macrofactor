# Answer: Stimulant Metabolism Interactions

## Scope Limitation

The SBS Diet skill is designed for evidence-based nutrition coaching focused on:

- Energy balance and calorie targets
- Protein, fat, and carbohydrate macronutrient management
- Diet patterns and meal timing
- Adherence and diet adjustments
- Micronutrients, fiber, and hydration

The question about caffeine, nicotine, and ADHD medication metabolism interactions is a **pharmacokinetics and drug-drug interaction question**, which falls outside the scope of the SBS Diet skill.

## Why This Question Cannot Be Answered Using SBS Diet

The SBS Diet skill contains no information about:

- Stimulant drug metabolism or clearance rates
- Cytochrome P450 enzyme interactions
- Pharmacokinetic interactions between caffeine and nicotine
- ADHD medication metabolism (amphetamines, methylphenidate, etc.)
- Drug-drug interaction mechanisms

## Appropriate Resource

This question requires consultation with:

- Pharmacology or pharmacokinetics literature
- Clinical drug interaction databases (e.g., DrugBank, FDA resources)
- A healthcare provider or pharmacist familiar with stimulant metabolism

The SBS Diet skill is not the appropriate tool for answering pharmacokinetic questions.
